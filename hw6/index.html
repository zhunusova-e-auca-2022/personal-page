About Women.kg

Our mission and purpose at AUCA

Fostering Empowerment and Growth

Women.kg is dedicated to supporting female students, faculty, and alumnae of the American University of Central Asia. We believe in creating a nurturing environment where women can thrive academically, professionally, and personally.

Our platform bridges the gap between opportunities and talent, offering a supportive space to share knowledge, experiences, and celebrate each other's achievements.

  • Dedicated support network
  • Access to exclusive resources
  • Professional mentorship
